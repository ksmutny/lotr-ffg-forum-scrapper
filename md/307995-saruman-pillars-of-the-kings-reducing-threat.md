I think that Saruman will interact unfavourably with the card. I think it's clear that in your example that the card is reducing your threat. The reason is on the card itself - you get an extra effect if the card raises your threat. If the card can raise your threat, then it can also reduceÂ it.

Presumably the reason the word reduce isn't on the card is because it can do either, unlike other similar cards that set threat to certain amounts.
